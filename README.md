# 🦈 Shark Attack Analysis

## 📌 Overview
Exploratory data analysis of the **Global Shark Attack dataset** using Python, with a focus on cleaning raw data and extracting insights from unstructured text.

---

## 🧰 Tools
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Regex (basic NLP)

---

## 🔍 What Was Analyzed

### 📈 Trends Over Time
- Shark attacks increase significantly after 1950  
- Likely driven by better reporting and population growth in coastal areas  

### 🌍 Locations
- Highest concentration of attacks in:
  - USA  
  - Australia  
  - South Africa  
- Certain coastal regions show consistent high risk  

### 🩹 Injuries (Text Analysis)
- Most affected body parts:
  - Legs and feet  
- Fatal cases are present but less frequent than non-fatal injuries  

### ⏰ Time of Day
- Most attacks occur during:
  - Afternoon  
  - Morning  
- Fewer incidents at night  

### 🦈 Shark Species
- Most involved species:
  - Great White  
  - Tiger  
  - Bull  
- Many records are uncertain or generic  

### 🏄 Activities
- Highest risk activities:
  - Surfing  
  - Swimming  
- Water surface activities show higher exposure  

---

## ⚙️ Key Steps
- Data cleaning (missing values, formatting)
- Feature engineering from text fields
- Grouping and aggregation for insights
- Data visualization
